Job Title: Mid-Level Network Engineer
Company: Cisco
Location: San Jose, CA (Hybrid)
Job Type: Full-time

Summary:
Cisco is seeking a talented and experienced Mid-Level Network Engineer to join our dynamic team. In this role, you will design, implement, and maintain complex network infrastructures for our global clients. You'll work with cutting-edge technologies and contribute to the evolution of networking solutions that power the internet and enterprise systems worldwide.

Responsibilities:
- Design and implement scalable, secure, and efficient network architectures
- Troubleshoot and resolve complex network issues in a timely manner
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to integrate network solutions with other IT systems
- Perform network upgrades and migrations with minimal downtime
- Develop and maintain network documentation, including diagrams and standard operating procedures
- Participate in on-call rotations to provide 24/7 support for critical network infrastructure
- Stay current with emerging networking technologies and industry best practices
- Mentor junior network engineers and contribute to knowledge sharing within the team

Minimum Requirements:
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Network Engineering, or a related technical field
- 3-5 years of hands-on experience in network engineering roles
- Strong understanding of networking protocols (TCP/IP, OSPF, BGP, EIGRP)
- Proficiency in configuring and maintaining Cisco routers, switches, and firewalls
- Experience with WAN technologies (MPLS, SD-WAN)
- Familiarity with network security principles and best practices
- Cisco CCNP certification or equivalent experience
- Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills
- Strong communication and teamwork abilities

Preferred Qualifications:
- Master's degree in a relevant field
- Experience with cloud networking (AWS, Azure, GCP)
- Knowledge of automation and orchestration tools (Ansible, Python)
- Familiarity with SDN (Software-Defined Networking) concepts
- Experience with network monitoring tools (SolarWinds, PRTG, Nagios)
- CCIE certification or in progress

Skills:
- Network Design and Implementation
- Cisco IOS and NX-OS
- Routing and Switching
- Network Security
- Troubleshooting
- WAN Technologies
- Network Automation
- Cloud Networking
- Documentation
- Project Management

Minimum Years of Experience: 3 years

Minimum Degree: B

Minimum GPA: Not specified
